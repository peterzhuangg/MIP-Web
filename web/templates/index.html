Papaya Medical Image Viewer   

Papaya is a pure open source JavaScript medical research image viewer, supporting DICOM and NIFTI formats. You can either upload your image from your device or use image in your file manager. This module can be accessed without Login. Source website: https://github.com/rii-mango/Papaya

FeTS Model - Federated Tumor Segmentation Challenge

Designed by Dr Kamlesh Pawar from Image Analysis Team. Aiming to robustly produce accurate brain tumor segmentations segmentation labels of the different glioma sub-regions across different medical institutions, locate and visualize segmentation of the different glioma sub-regions： “enhancing tumor” (ET)， “tumor core” (TC) and “whole tumor” (WT).
